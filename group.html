Group Loan

Introduction

SHRI G FINANCE Group Loan is designed to extend loan facilities to women, with the objective of helping them meet their financial needs.

Features and Benefits
  • Loan amount: ranging from Rs 15,000 thousand to Rs 80,000
  • Age of Applicant: Between 18 to 58 years
  • Loan Tenure: Maximum 24 months
  • Security: Group guarantee
  • Processing Fee :0 - 2% plus applicable GST
  • Flexible part prepayment options
  • Interest Rates :Enjoy highly competitive interest rates ranging from 20% - 24% p.a.
How to Apply
  • Visit our website our Loan Executive will guide you on the application process
  • Contact us and our Customer Care Executive will fix a doorstep service
  • appointment with our Loan Executive to help you with the application process.
Eligibility
  • Applicants must meet the following requirements to be eligible for the loan:
  • Should fulfill KYC documents requirements
  • Age should be between 18 to 58 years
  • Should not have an adverse credit record
  • Should meet the acceptable credit norms according to SHRI G FINANCE
  • Should be part of a group of customers who are willing to guarantee each other for the repayment of loan
Documentation

Here is an indicative list of documents that will be required

  • Primary ID: Aadhaar
  • Secondary ID: PAN card / Form 60
  • In case of non-availability of PAN card, customer needs to submit Form 60 along with the one of the following OVDs (Officially Valid Documents)
  • Passport
  • Voter ID card
  • Driving licence
  • NREGA (National Rural Employment Guarantee Act)
  • Letter issued by National Population Register containing details of name and address
  • Residence Proof: Any of the valid KYC documents Utility bill (not more than 60 days old) For Terms and Conditions, please contact the SHRI G FINANCE department Service Charges & Fees
