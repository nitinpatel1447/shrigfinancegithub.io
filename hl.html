Home Loan

Introduction

Are you looking to Finance your Dream Home? Your wait is over!!SHRI G FINANCE Presents Home Loans, which allows you to borrow a substantial amount of money to Finance that Dream House of your choice,SHRI G FINANCE will fund you the desired loan amount basis your eligibility.

Features and Benefits
  • We have a simple online application process
  • Fill our simple application: It takes a few minutes to fill our online application form
  • EMI based loansSubmit your documents: Our representative will visit you to collect your documents.
  • Verification Our representative may get in touch with you to verify your details and may also schedule a personal discussion
  • Approval On successful verification, your loan amount would be approved and money will be deposited in your bank account
To get approval, you will need
  • Name & Date of Birth
  • Identity Documents
  • Latest Income Tax Returns
  • Last 6 months bank statements
  • Property Papers
Eligibility
  • Sole Proprietors and Partnership Firms
  • Micro Small and Medium Enterprises
  • Salaried and Self Employed Professionals
  • You need to be at least 2 years in business (for Self-employed) or have 3 years
  • work experience (for salaried individuals) in an industry with a positive net worth
  • The age requirement is minimum 21 years of age at the time of taking the loan and maximum of 60 years of age or retirement at the time of maturity
  • Clear banking and loan payment history
  • Good credit rating score in credit bureau
Documentation

Here is an indicative list of documents that will be required

  • Audited Financials
  • IT Returns
  • Form 16 A
  • Bank Statements
  • KYC
  • Ownership Proof
